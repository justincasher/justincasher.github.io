## 1. Classical Cartan-Hadamard Theorem

**Cartan-Hadamard Theorem.** &emsp; Let $$ M $$ be a complete, connected Riemannian manifold of nonpositive curvature. Fix a point $$ p $$. Then the exponential $$ \exp_p \colon T_p M \to M $$ is a universal cover.
